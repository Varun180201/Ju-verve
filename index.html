About the Fest

Out with the third version of this National Level sports event, we strive for eagerness and commitment in everything we do. Sports is our passion and joy. Passion for our work is the fuel that inspires us to achieve astounding results. An extravagant event conducted in collaboration with STUDENT COUNCIL (JU EAGLES) will have staggering sports continued till night to give a feast to your eyes and have a whale of a time. We have believed sports have the power to change lives. Whether it’s the friendships made in American Football, a bruise got in Kabaddi or the exhaustion after a run that puts everything into perspective, the impacts of these experiences are what JU VERVE is all about.

Rule for Football JU Verve'2023


FOOTBALL


1. Matches shall be played according to the rules of FIFA as adopted from time to time by the All India Football Federation unless otherwise modified.


2. All matches will be in knockout phase.


3. The duration of each match shall be 60 minutes. (30-5-30)


4. In case of draw between teams, two periods of extra time will be played (each extra time of 10 minutes with 2 minutes break). If still draw situation occurs, penalty shootouts shall occur.


5. Every team shall submit a list of players, not exceeding 15, who may participate in the tournament. Not more than 15 certificates shall be awarded to a team.


6. There will be 9 players on the field per team at a time.


7. A team can substitute a maximum of 5 players in a match.


ENTRY FEES PER TEAM - ₹3100


Rule for Cricket JU Verve'2023


Cricket General Rules:


• The umpire’s decision will be final decision.


• All teams have to report 30 minutes prior to match time.


• Names of team players should be provided 30 minutes before match.


• All players must bring their college ID card.


• Every team is expected to follow tournament rules.


Tournament Rules:


• All the rules of International Cricket will be applicable.


• Matches will be of 10 overs each.


• All matches will be played on Knockout Basis.


• No person shall play for more than one team in the tournament.


• Teams must maintain discipline on and off the field during the tournament.


• All matches will be played with Red Leather Ball.


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification.


Team Size - 15 max

Entry fees - ₹3100


Rule for VolleyBall JU Verve'2023


Volleyball General Rules:


• The rules of the International Volleyball Federation [FIVB] as adopted from time to time by the Volleyball Federation of India (VFI) shall be followed unless otherwise modified.


• Every team can have 14 players maximum . Not more than 14 certificates shall be awarded to a team.


• The tournament shall be played on knockout basis.


• All players must bring their college ID card.


• Team will rotate each time they win the serve. Players shall rotate in a clockwise manner.


• Players must be in volleyball uniform (shorts and jersey).


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification.


Team Size - 12 max

Entry fees - ₹2100


Rule for BasketBall JU Verve'2023


Basketball General Rules:


• The tournament will be conducted according to the International Basketball Federation Rules as adopted from time to time by the Indian Basketball Federation unless otherwise modified.


• The number of players representing any university will not be more than 14 in case of men and not more than 14 certificates be awarded to a team.


• Tournament is in knock out format.


• All players must bring their college ID card.


• During matches if the score is same after the end of the scheduled time, Basketball Federation Rules will be applied, i.e. the game shall continue with as many extra periods of 5 minutes as is necessary to break the tie.


• Initial possession : Coin flip


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification.


Length of game:

  1. First half: 20 minutes running time
  2. Half-time: 1 minutes
  3. Second half: 20 minutes running time / last 2 minutes stop time if scores are within a 10 point difference.
  4. Time-Outs: 2 per game (regulation time)


Team Size - 12 max

Entry fees - ₹2100


Rule for Kabaddi JU Verve'2023


Kabaddi General Rules:


• The rules of the Amateur Kabaddi Federation Of India shall be followed.


• The team that wins the toss will have the choice to elect either to rai or the side of the half. The team which looses the toss will have the remaining choice.


• In the second half of the game, the sides of the play field will be changed.


• All players must bring their college ID card.


• The team, which did not, start the game with raid in the first half, will start the game with raid in the second half of the game. The game in the second half will resume with the same number of players, as they were at the end of the first half.


• Duration of the match 40 minutes divided in two halves of 20 minutes each with a break of 5 minutes in between two halves.


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification.


Team Size - 10 max

Entry fees - ₹2100


Rule for American Football (Flag) JU Verve'2023


American Football (Flag)
General Rules:


• The rules of American Football Federation of India shall be followed.


• Each team will have 8 playing players .


• All passes must be forward and received beyond the line of scrimmage.


• All players must bring their college ID card.


• Only direct handoffs are permitted—there are no laterals or pitches anywhere on the field.


• The quarterback has seven-second pass clock to get rid of the ball. The quarterback can’t run with the ball unless it was handed off first.


• Offensive players must steer clear of the rusher and may not get in his/her way.


• Any defensive player lined up seven yards off the line of scrimmage is eligible to rush If the ball is handed off, any defender may rush.


• Interceptions are returnable (even on extra point attempts)


• All offensive flag football penalties result in a loss of down and yardage.


• The ball is dead when it hits the ground, the offensive player’s flag is pulled from their belt, the ball-carrier steps out of bounds, or the ball- carrier’s body—outside of their hands or feet—touches the ground


• All defensive flag football penalties result in an automatic first down and some are associated with yardage.


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification.


Team Size - 14 max

Entry fees - ₹2100


Rule for Futsal (Girls) JU Verve'2023


Futsal Girls


• There’ll be a total of 8 players in each team playing on a size of 5-a-side football pitch.


• Wearing any ornaments is strictly prohibited.


• Each match will be of 33 minutes in total, leading with 15 minutes halves and a 3 minutes break in between.


• A cautionary yellow card will be shown to the player in case of any offense.


• Double yellow cards or a red card for the serious offences which will lead to the discontinuation of the player’s involvement further in the match.


• Double red cards will lead to player’s disqualification in the finals


• Normal outside and corner kicks are allowed


• There are No offsides


• In case of tie, 5 penalties will be announced to each team


• There’ll be knock out matches


• All players must wear the same colour jerseys other than the keeper. The keeper’s Jersey must be of different color than therest of his teammates.


• Any kind of misbehaviour will lead to direct disqualification


Team Size - 8 Max

Entry Fees - ₹1500
